About Nyturals

Nyturals is a formulation-driven cosmetic company rooted in a legacy that emerged from Dharamdas Tirathdas & Co., established in 1925.

Built on decades of formulation expertise, Nyturals combines scientific precision with traditional understanding to create premium soaps, syndet bars, and skin care systems.

The company offers manufacturing, brand building, consultancy, and transparent soap base development from scratch.
